About Us
DentistryIQ is a leading source of information to help promote oral health and well-being by supporting both dental professionals and empowered consumers.
Since 2009, we have published excellent content for dentists, dental practice owners, dental hygienists, dental office
managers, dental assistants, and dental school students.

As public awareness of the importance of oral health and dental health has grown, we have grown into offering accurate,
helpful information for anyone.
Dental professionals will find content on patient treatment, technology updates, dental equipment, patient communication, dental coding,
dental insurance, practice management, ergonomics, human resources issues, dental marketing, new dental products, product reviews, public
health, dental service organizations, dental office design, oral-systemic health, implantology, orthodontics, infection control, ergonomics,
pharmacology, hygiene therapies, and more.

We also feature information about dental health, gum disease, oral hygiene, dental pain, Invisalign and braces, and more.

In partnership with DIQ’s sister publications—Dental Economics, RDH magazine, and Perio-Implant Advisory—and sister conferences—RDH Under One Roof and the Principles of Practice Management Conference—DIQ strives to guide dental professionals via content provided by
